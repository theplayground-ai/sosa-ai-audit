How We Scored This
These scores are based on a technical audit of SOSA's publicly visible infrastructure as of April 2026:
Lead Capture + CRM (10%): Expedition interest goes through a Google Form with no automated follow-up, no lead scoring, and no CRM. The 10% reflects that the form exists, but there is zero automation behind it.
Email Automation (5%): No visible email sequences, welcome flows, or drip campaigns. No newsletter signup on the website. The 5% accounts for basic Shopify order confirmation emails only.
Content Workflow (30%): Strong content creation (Blake and team produce high-quality field footage and stories). The 30% reflects the human effort, but there is no AI-assisted scheduling, repurposing, or cross-platform automation.
DM + Engagement (5%): With 451K followers on Blake's personal account and growing on @the.sosa, all DM responses are manual. No auto-responders, no comment-to-DM funnels, no chatbot. 5% for the human effort of responding.
Booking + Ops (15%): Shopify handles merch transactions. Expedition booking is a Google Form with manual coordination. No automated scheduling, payment plans, or waitlist management. 15% for the Shopify layer.
Community + Alumni (10%): The SOS Alliance page exists on the website but has no portal, no membership system, and no alumni engagement automation. 10% for the stated intent and the strong 60% return rate (proof the community wants to stay connected).
